Fierce

Fierce is a multidisciplinary exhibition and related symposium organized at Parsons The New School for Design in November 2013, exploring fashion culture by means of contemporary art, design and media. The exhibited works comment on the unsustainability of the fashion industry, investigate how people use clothes as tools for building their identities, and analyze the relationship of fashion and corporeality.
